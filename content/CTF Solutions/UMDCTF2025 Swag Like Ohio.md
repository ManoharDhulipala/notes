## Solution

When zooming in to the center, there is a clock tower that might be popular. I decided to screenshot the photo and submit it to the yandex search image. Below is the screenshot I entered in yandex.

![](https://lh7-rt.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfTKVqLnc_AwTiFu2pvWzLmR2OJ5FmYpEssg6XZ_HZChMe9R7qP89cCRNi9tbcx8YLZfhaPUuemH_hi-fOa2RR0EAGI55v8OSw5j6pd3Vnj5MJiCJJuXOvEFxjdo9htb0HuuIbYLg?key=ZYzkjSX7e_gszKWKg1GUH5mc)

The [result](https://yandex.com/images/search?cbir_id=3789817%2Fwy0Xj1lPdVbHOexWa_hh7Q367&cbir_page=similar&lr=102646&rpt=imageview&url=https%3A%2F%2Favatars.mds.yandex.net%2Fget-images-cbir%2F3789817%2Fwy0Xj1lPdVbHOexWa_hh7Q367%2Forig) of the image search pointed to an image in wikimedia matching the clock tower showing Putnam street. When pulling it up on Google Maps, I found the bridge close by named Putnam bridge. Below is the exact solution

`UMD{Putnam Bridge, Marietta, OH 45750}`
